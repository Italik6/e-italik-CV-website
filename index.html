About Me

I am a Web Developer. I started my adventure in this field on studies in Cracow. At the beginning it was just a hobby. I signed up for studies at Front-End Development. I did not even suspect that the professional process of creating websites can be so exciting. For several months, I spent every spare moment on improving my skills and learning new technologies. I have focused on the fast advancement because I see my future in this area. I set up the company and day after day I undertake more serious web projects. I can safely say that the world of HTML, CSS and JS pulled me in with no hope of a quick return.

Although when I manage to break free from the clutches (in case when my computer breaks down or electromagnetic storm disrupts the connection to the Internet) I get on the playing field. I play football and volleyball and after such a challenging activity I necessarily hit up a pub with my friends, where I turn into a clamorous fan of the Premier League and further support my sports emotions. When the emotions finally subside, www world is already getting to me again...
